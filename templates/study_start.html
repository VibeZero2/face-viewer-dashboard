Study Instructions

Thank you for agreeing to participate in our research on face perception and trust. In this study, you will:

  1. View a series of facial images
  2. Rate each face on trustworthiness using a scale from 1 (not at all trustworthy) to 7 (very trustworthy)
  3. Optionally provide brief explanations for your ratings

There are no right or wrong answers. We are interested in your genuine perceptions and judgments.

Important Notes:
  • The study will take approximately 15-20 minutes to complete
  • Please complete the study in one session
  • Try to minimize distractions during the study
  • Your responses are confidential and will be used for research purposes only
